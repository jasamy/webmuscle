Who we are

Webmuscle is the work of UK-based designer / developer Jason Byers.

You can contact Jason on 07595 454727 or via the contact page.

@jasonleebyers

 Follow me on Twitter

Jason was born in the small town of Mossend, Scotland.

After leaving school he went to work as a junior finished artist at a small advertising agency in Glasgow.

It was from there that he studied design at Glasgow College of Printing.

With the advent of the Apple Macintosh and desktop publishing, Jason moved to London to work in the corporate publications department for a large advertising agency.

During his time in London Jason worked for a small design/reprographics company specialising in the fashion and music industry.

It was here where he honed his skills in typography and image manipulation as well as gaining his first exposure to the world-wide-web.

After 10 years in London Jason moved back to Scotland and joined a large Glasgow-based media company.

Very quickly he became involved in the online side of the business and helped it grow from a small web-presence to a globally recognised brand.

Jason then worked for a web-based start-up making short educational films for schools.

Charged with taking the companies unique content online, Jason together with a small team of designers and developers created a multi-award winning website which has been sucessfully received world-wide.
